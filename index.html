How to Log In to Your MacPaw Account

MacPaw offers some of the most popular and reliable macOS utilities like CleanMyMac X, Setapp, and Gemini. Whether you're a new user or long-time customer, logging into your MacPaw account allows you to manage your software licenses, subscriptions, and support requests from one centralized dashboard.

Why Create a MacPaw Account?

Creating a MacPaw account is essential for managing your purchases and subscriptions efficiently. With an account, you can:

  • Access and download purchased software
  • View and manage licenses
  • Update billing and payment methods
  • Access customer support and knowledge base
  • Keep your software up-to-date

Step-by-Step: Logging In

Follow these steps to securely log in to your MacPaw account:

  1. Go to the official login page at https://macpawaccount.com
  2. Enter your registered email address.
  3. Input your password and click the "Login" button.
  4. Optionally, you can log in using Google or Apple ID for faster access.

Forgot Your Password?

If you've forgotten your password, simply click the “Forgot password?” link on the login page. You will receive an email with instructions to reset your password securely.

Troubleshooting Login Issues

Here are common reasons users may experience login problems, along with quick solutions:

  • Incorrect Email/Password: Double-check your credentials for typos.
  • Browser Issues: Clear your browser cache or try an alternative browser.
  • Disabled Cookies: Make sure cookies and JavaScript are enabled in your browser settings.
  • Slow Internet: Ensure a stable internet connection.

Account Security Tips

To keep your account secure, follow these best practices:

  • Use a strong, unique password that combines letters, numbers, and symbols.
  • Do not share your login credentials with anyone.
  • Enable two-factor authentication if available.
  • Always log out of your account when using shared or public computers.

Benefits of Staying Logged In

Contacting MacPaw Support
